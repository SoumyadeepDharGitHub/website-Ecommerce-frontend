The one stop for all things handmade.Keeping alive the old traditions of Bengali craftmanship alive.
Our products are made 100% by hand using self sourced raw products, turning them into the a work of art by highly skilled artisans using the traditional ways.

Our moto "Women empowerment through employment"

Our platform helps artisans (male/female/small business) to join our journey in bringing the handicraft industry into the digital world. We strongly belive in women empowerment hence we provide a work from home option for women who for some reason are unable to work in a manufacturing facility. The raw products are delivered to the house and end products are sourced back to the main facility after completion. Hence providing them financial independancy.
